Help & Support

Getting started

How do I generate an image?

Tap the + button in the center of the tab bar, choose Image Generation, select an AI model, enter your prompt, and tap Generate. The result appears in your History when ready.

How do I generate a video?

Tap +, choose Video Generation, pick a model, write your prompt, configure duration and resolution, and tap Generate. Video generation takes longer than images — keep the app open until it completes.

Can I use a reference image?

Yes — many models support an optional reference photo. Tap Add Photo inside the generation screen to pick one from your library or take one with your camera. Granting photo library or camera permission is required.

What AI models are available?

Aura includes models from fal.ai (Flux, Wan, Kling, Veo, Pika, Hailuo, PixVerse, and more) and Google Gemini. The Home feed shows all available models with coin costs and PRO requirements.

Coins & Aura PRO

What are coins?

Coins are the in-app currency for generating content. Each model shows its coin cost before you generate. You start with a small free coin balance, and can buy more or subscribe to PRO for a monthly coin allowance.

What does PRO include?

  • A monthly coin allowance refreshed every billing period.
  • Access to PRO-tier AI models not available on the free plan.
  • Priority generation queue.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

In the App, go to Account → Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Aura, then tap Cancel Subscription. Your PRO features remain active until the end of the current billing period. Cancelling does not delete your data.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select the Aura charge.

I ran out of coins mid-generation — will I be charged?

Coins are reserved before a generation starts. If you don't have enough coins, the generation will not start and no coins are deducted. If a generation fails due to a provider error, your coins are automatically refunded.

Account & data

Do I need to create an account?

No. Aura uses an anonymous account created automatically when you first launch the App. Your history and coins are linked to this account on your device. No email or password is required.

What happens if I reinstall the App?

Your anonymous account is tied to your device. Uninstalling the App may make your generation history and coin balance unavailable on that device. If you have an active PRO subscription, you can restore it via Restore Purchases on any device with the same Apple ID.

How do I delete my account and data?

See the Account & Data Deletion page for full instructions. You can also delete from Account → Delete Account inside the App.

Generation issues

My generation failed.

If a generation fails due to a provider error, your coins are automatically refunded. Try again with a different prompt or model. If failures persist, please email support with the model name and a description of your prompt.

The result doesn't look right.

AI generation is non-deterministic. Try refining your prompt with more detail, adjusting parameters (size, duration), or using a different model. The Home feed shows example results for each model to help calibrate expectations.

Generation is taking too long.

Video generation can take several minutes depending on model and resolution. Keep the App open — leaving the App in the background may interrupt polling. If a generation never completes, wait a few minutes and check your History; in rare cases you may need to try again.

I can't upload a photo.

Make sure you have granted Aura access to your photo library or camera in iOS Settings → Aura → Photos / Camera.
