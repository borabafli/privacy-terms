Account & Data Deletion

You are always in control of your data.

Aura lets you permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from inside the App. No email or support ticket is required. This page exists to comply with Apple App Store guideline 5.1.1(v) and to give you full transparency over how deletion works.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, your generation history, generated images, generated videos, and coin balance are erased and cannot be recovered. Cancelling or refunding a subscription must be handled separately through Apple.

Delete your account from the App

  1. Open Aura on your iPhone.
  2. Tap the profile icon (top-right of the Home screen) to open the Account screen.
  3. Scroll to the Data Management section.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Read the confirmation dialog and confirm. You will be signed out and all your data will be erased immediately.

What gets deleted

  • Your anonymous account and its unique identifier.
  • Your coin balance and all transaction history.
  • All generated images and videos stored in cloud storage.
  • All generation metadata (prompts, model used, timestamps, output URLs).
  • All locally cached generation history on the device where you confirmed deletion.

What is not deleted automatically

  • App Store subscription: if you have an active Aura PRO subscription, you must cancel it separately at iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Aura. Apple does not allow developers to cancel subscriptions on your behalf. Cancelling your subscription does not refund any remaining subscription period.
  • Local files saved to your camera roll: any images or videos you saved to your iOS Photos library are stored by iOS, not Aura. These remain in your Photos app and are not deleted when you delete your Aura account.
  • Limited billing records: certain billing and tax records may be retained by Apple and by us for up to 7 years to comply with accounting and tax obligations. These records do not contain the content of your prompts or generated images.
  • Support emails: if you have emailed support, those threads remain in our inbox for up to 24 months. Reply asking us to delete your support history and we will do so.

How to also cancel your subscription

  1. Open Settings on your device.
  2. Tap your name at the top.
  3. Tap Subscriptions.
  4. Select Aura and tap Cancel Subscription.

Can't open the App?

If you cannot use the in-app delete flow (for example, your device is broken or lost), email borabafli@gmail.com from the email address associated with the Apple ID that purchased Aura coins or a PRO subscription. Include the approximate date of your last use and the device model. We will verify your request and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Because Aura uses anonymous accounts (no email sign-up), we identify accounts by the RevenueCat customer ID linked to your Apple ID — so having made at least one purchase is the best way for us to verify your identity remotely.
